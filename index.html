Welcome to the Village of Fjällnäs

About Us

Nestled at the foot of snow-capped peaks and surrounded by ancient pine forests, the village of Fjällnäs is a quiet retreat from the hustle and bustle of the world. With its cobblestone streets, cozy wooden cabins, and tranquil lakeside views, Fjällnäs feels like a place suspended in time—a perfect balance of nature’s untouched beauty and the warmth of community.

Grounds & Glory Founded in 1967 is located at the heart of Fjällnäs, a charming little coffee shop that mirrors the village's peaceful and minimalist vibe. The shop sits by the village’s sparkling blue lake, framed by towering evergreens and dramatic mountain ranges. Here, you’ll find the scent of freshly ground coffee beans mingling with the crisp, clean air—creating a perfect escape from everyday life.Whether you're wandering in after a morning hike in the mountains or seeking a warm refuge from the crisp air, Grounds & Glory offers a welcoming space to pause and recharge. With its wooden interior, soft wool throws, and warm fireside, this little café is as much about a feeling of tranquility as it is about great coffee. Locals and travelers alike gather here for more than just a drink—they come for a sense of connection to the land and each other.
